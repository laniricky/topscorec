
# 🏢 Topscore Consultants Website

A modern, responsive business website for **Topscore Consultants Limited** — built with **HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and JavaScript**.  
The site showcases company information, services, testimonials, and contact details, providing a professional online presence.

---

## 📌 Features
- ✅ Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- ✅ Homepage with service highlights and slider
- ✅ About, Services, and Contact pages
- ✅ Preloader and smooth navigation
- ✅ Testimonials & client partnerships section
- ✅ Contact form with validation
- ✅ Integrated social media icons (Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram)
